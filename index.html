Treasure Valley Trails Committee

Welcome to the Treasure Valley Trails Committee's tiny site on Github.

Remember that Treasure Valley Scout Reservation is private property and access is by permission only; youth safety is of utmost importance.

This site primarily serves our trail map, which currently shows all trails and roads as well as property lines and abutting open space. Clicking on the PNG image below will load a high-resolution PDF.

Map is developed by Brett Russ on behalf of the Trails Committee using GIS data version controlled in our TVSR GIS Github repo. Note that the GIS data is private to the organization at this time..
